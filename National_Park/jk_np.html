National Park In Jammu & Kashmir


Name : Dachigam National Park

Country : India

State : Jammu & Kashmir

Details :Dachigam National Park is located 22 kilometers from Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir, on the east side of Dal Lake. It covers an area of 141 km2. The name of the park literally stands for "ten villages" which is in the memory of the ten villages that were relocated for its formation. These ten villages were living in this region before the World War I the beginning of the early 20th century. The main gate entrance is very close to the New Theed general bus stand on the either side of Darul Uloom Kousaria.
