National Park In Andhra Pradesh


Name : Sri Venkateshwara National Park

Country : India

State : Andhra Pradesh

Details : Sri Venkateswara National Park is a national park and biosphere reserve in Andhra Pradesh, India.
The total area of the park is 353 km2. The park is known for its many waterfalls, including the Talakona, Gundalakona and Gunjana. As the government of India declared the Seshachalam Hills as one of the biosphere reserves of India in 2010, this national park becomes the part of it.

Name : Leopard

Country : India

State : Andhra Pradesh

Animal Description : leopard, (Panthera pardus), , also called panther, large cat closely related to the lion, tiger, and jaguar. The name leopard was originally given to the cat now called cheetah—the so-called hunting leopard—which was once thought to be a cross between the lion and the pard. The term pard was eventually replaced by the name leopard.
