National Park In Bihar


Name : Valmiki National Park

Country : India

State : Bihar

Details :Valmiki National Park is a Tiger Reserve in the West Champaran District of Bihar, India.[1] It is the only national park in Bihar.[2] Valmiki Tiger Reserve covers 898.45 km2 (346.89 sq mi), which is 17.4% of the total geographical area of the district. As of 2018, there were 40 tigers in the Reserve.
